The Chef — Kei Koo

“Clean and pure — yet layered.”

Born in Hong Kong, Kei Koo built his craft on French ground: formed at two-MICHELIN-star Amber, sous chef at Écriture as it earned two stars within eight months of opening, then co-chef of Taipei's one-star T+T — and laureate of the MICHELIN Young Chef Award 2022.

In April 2023 he took the helm of de nuit. His contemporary French leans neither on rare produce nor on technique for its own sake: seasonal ingredients, sauces of quiet depth, and a lightness that never raises its voice.
